# FormPilot — Chrome Extension

Auto-fill any form with your company data. YC applications, vendor forms, RFPs—done in seconds.

## Install

1. Open **chrome://extensions**, turn on **Developer mode**.
2. Click **Load unpacked** and select the `formpilot` folder (this repo: `apps/formpilot`).
3. Click the FormPilot icon on any form page → **Edit company data** → add your company name, contact, email, etc. and **Save**.

## Use

- Open a page with a form.
- Click the FormPilot icon → **Fill form**.
- Fields are filled from your company data.

That’s it. No backend or account required to get started.
